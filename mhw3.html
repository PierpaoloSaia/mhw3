The 5 best players of the moment

Last update: today

1.

Kylian Mbappé

Mbappé was born as an offensive winger in football, but he is also able to fill the role of first striker. Very skilled in dribbling, prolific finisher, he is equipped with great speed and tactical intelligence, which allow him to leave behind his direct marker and, consequently, be incisive in depth. For his style of play, he has been compared to compatriot Thierry Henry.
Explosive.
2.

Erling Haaland

He is a striker with good technique, speed and physical strength. He has an excellent nose for goal, is left-footed, and his 194 cm allows him to be a good header. The power of his shots makes them insidious and he is also good at entering the spaces left by the opposing defenses. He can also distinguish himself as a finisher thanks to his good vision of the game. He is inspired by Zlatan Ibrahimović.
Alien.
3.

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia

Left wing with a slender physique, he can play on the entire offensive front. Despite being good with both feet, he prefers to use his right foot, especially to centralize starting from the opposite side. Elegant in running, he has great acceleration with the ball, as well as being skilled in dribbling, finishing and set pieces. He gives his best when he is in the open field, and is also able to create scoring chances for his teammates, thanks to his good vision of the game. Due to his characteristics, he is considered one of the best talents ever expressed by Georgian football.
Unstoppable.
4.

Jude Bellingham

Dynamic player and skilled in dribbling in narrow spaces, he is a midfielder who combines good physicality with excellent dribbling skills.
Fundamental.
5.

Phil Foden

Left-handed attacking midfielder, gifted with excellent individual technique, thanks to his tactical flexibility he can also act as a winger or false nueve. Hailed as one of the best prospects in world football, he said he was inspired by former teammate David Silva.
Sublime.
